About Me

I currently serve on the board of Maine Transgender Network, a peer support network for transgender residents of Maine. In my role as Chair of the Communications and Development Committee, I am responsible for the development of our branded imagery, management of our communications calendar, and maintainance of our websites. After working in development and communications for several nonprofits, I am excited to branch away from marketing and social media in my studies as a Full Stack Developer. With a Bachelors in Studio Art, I have a keen eye for design and look foward to utlizing my creative skills in my work as a Full Stack Developer.

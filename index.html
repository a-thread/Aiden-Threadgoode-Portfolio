About Me

I am currently serving on the board of Maine Transgender Network as Chair of the Communications and Development Work Committee. My primary responsibilities include the development of our branding, management of our communications calendar, not to mention the restructuring and maintainance of our websites. I have worked in development and communications for nonprofits over two years and I'm excited to branch away from the marketing and social media aspects of those positions in my studies as a Full Stack Developer. With a Bachelors in Studio Art, I have a keen eye for design and look foward to getting more familiar with front-end web development.
